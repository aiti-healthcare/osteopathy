Osteopathy

Osteopathy is a hands-on approach to healthcare that views the body as one connected system. It uses gentle techniques to improve how the body moves and functions. The idea is that when structures like joints, muscles, nerves, organs, and blood flow are well balanced, the body can maintain better health.

Osteopathic Manual Practitioner

Osteopathic Manual Practitioners use methods such as gentle joint movements, stretching, and soft tissue techniques to treat issues like back, neck, and shoulder pain. By improving mobility, reducing tension, and supporting circulation, osteopathy helps the body’s natural healing processes work more effectively.
