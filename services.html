Osteoarticular Joint Mobilization

Joint mobilizations help restore normal movement in a joint that has been limited by injury or disease, and can also help reduce pain through gentle, controlled movements. It is a safe and effective part of osteopathic treatment.

Muscle Energy Techniques

Muscle Energy Techniques is an active, direct manual osteopathic technique which promotes voluntary contraction against controlled counterforce from precise position and specific direction. It stretches, strengthens, and relaxes muscles while reduces spasm, hypertonicity, pain and prevents injury.

Therapeutic Exercises

Therapeutic Exercises includes different stretching techniques which helps restore range of motion rapidly, improve flexibility, speed up healing by enhancing blood circulation of the area and reduce inflammation. It also prevents deleterious effects of prolonged immobilization.
